How to Handle Losing at Pickleball

A practical guide for emotional recovery after an unexpected defeat

Step 1: Take a Deep Breath

Losing at pickleball can feel shocking, especially when you were “definitely winning” up until the score proved otherwise.

Pro Tip: Counting to ten is effective. Counting to eleven is suspiciously competitive.

Step 2: Accept Reality (Briefly)

  • “Good game.”
  • “Nice shots.”
  • “Yeah, you played well today.”

Step 3: Do Not Blame the Paddle

Paddles do not lose pop. Players lose patience.

Warning: Complaining about a $200 paddle reduces credibility by 73%.
